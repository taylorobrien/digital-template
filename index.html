Falling Roses

  • High Concept: You have lost memory and are gaining roses to remember your loved one.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player should feel the game simple, but frustrating when the potion flies quickly out of the sky to decrease their score. 
  • Genre: Magical-catching game.
  • Game play: In this prototype of Falling Roses, the player may move back and forth to collect roses. The player is intended to get to a score of 1000 to regain their memory. The potions collected will subtract a great amount of points from the player depending on how long they are hovered over the potion for.
  • Prototype goal: This prototype is evaluating falling objects, object collision, score keeping, and simple movements.
  • Features: The storyline.
  • Setting/Premise: The player is in a dark and gloomy graveyard, collecting bright and colorful roses to gain his memory back of his lost loved one. 
  • Story: The character, Charlie, has been cursed by a witch and lost his memory of his loved one. He must collect roses in order to regain his memory of his loved one back. While trying, the witch continues to throw potions at Charlie attempting to pull him further away from his love.
  • Target Audience: This game is intended for new-gamers, or gamers that enjoy simple games.
  • Play time: 1-4 minutes.
  • Strategies: Avoiding potions, collecting roses.
